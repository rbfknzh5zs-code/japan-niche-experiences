Why can't I book these campsites myself?

Most Japanese campsites only accept phone reservations in Japanese. They don't have English websites or international booking systems. Even popular sites like Fumotoppara require Japanese-language phone calls to secure a spot.

How much is your agency fee?

What if my preferred dates aren't available?

We'll immediately let you know and suggest alternative dates or similar campsites. You're never charged until we secure a confirmed booking. If we can't book your request, there's no fee.

Do you provide camping equipment?

We're a booking agency, not a rental company. However, many campsites offer equipment rental, and we can arrange this when we make your reservation. Just let us know what you need in your request.

How do I pay?

After we confirm your booking, we'll send payment instructions. You can pay via credit card or bank transfer. The campsite fee goes to the campsite; our agency fee goes to us. Simple and transparent.
