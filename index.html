FAQ

Common Questions

What is a digital detox package?

Our packages combine glamping accommodation with curated nature experiences — onsen, forest bathing, BBQ, and more. Everything is prepared so you can simply arrive, put your phone away, and reconnect with nature. We handle all Japanese communication and logistics.

What's included in each package?

Every package includes glamping accommodation, a BBQ set, an English PDF guide (with campsite rules, garbage sorting instructions), an onsen map of the area, and a directions video. Higher-tier packages add forest bathing, meditation guides, craft beer, private onsen, stargazing equipment, and more.

Do I need camping experience?

Not at all. Our packages use comfortable glamping facilities — think real beds, heating, and proper amenities. The English PDF guide covers everything you need to know, from check-in procedures to garbage sorting. We've removed every barrier so you can just enjoy nature.

Will I have phone signal / WiFi?

Most locations have limited or no cell service — that's part of the experience. Some glamping facilities offer WiFi at reception. We provide all directions and information in advance so you don't need connectivity once you arrive. Emergency contact options are always available.

Is everything in English?

Yes. Every package includes an English PDF guide, directions video, and onsen map. We handle all Japanese-language communication — reservations, local coordination, everything. You also get support via email or LINE throughout your trip.
